Les bons vivants – Le trottoir d’en face

The good old days

Who are they?

This music which is called "les bons vivants" and which comes to us from the group Le trottoir d’en face, presents us a band of friends they come from Orthez, it is a beautiful small country village in which all these friends appreciate their life and nothing can make them happier than their music, their small country village and the good moments which they share all together .

The reality of life

I find this music captivating, I share the opinion of his eight friends, that the most beautiful thing to live is not to stay behind social networks all day or to stay locked up at home, but to go out and meet all your friends to share a good meal and then discuss everything and anything.

For me, this is how I enjoy my life the best, after all everyone is free to enjoy life as they feel. I'm quite an amateur, but I think that this music makes us aware that enjoying our loved ones can be one of the most beautiful things we can live, we have to make compromises in order not to get stuck in the virtual world, but also not to be completely disconnected from the technological world.

I often listen to this music when I'm just at a country party with my friends, we typically experience the same moments that the pavement across the street has implemented in their video. It allows us to get out of our bubble and think about something else than all the problems we face in our daily lives. The emotions that this music gives me are much better than the ones I can have behind my phone screen. So that's why I chose this music.
