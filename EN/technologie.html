Hope

An important issue

My generation's technology

In 2022, the evolution of technology is something that has already made a real impact and at least at the moment it is something that I find remarkable. My generation has been lucky enough to grow up with advanced technology from a very young age. I think it's really impressive what we are able to invent and innovate. It's amazing to see how cars, computers and all sorts of things have evolved in such a short time. And I'm really curious to see how technology will continue to evolve over the years.

Important innovations

What will be the next innovations in cars, planes, telephones and so on? And what will be the new feats that human beings will develop in the years to come. Will we all be able to go into space thanks to the advances of NASA, or will there be flying cars, and maybe one day we will even be able to teleport to different places in the world. Well, good for now, we're not at that level of technology yet, but considering that it never stops surprising us, maybe I'll be able to enjoy all the new things in my lifetime. Anyway, that's how I see the future so far.

What will it bring to the human being?

I hope that the new technology will not help to limit human relationships, but rather improve the relationships of all of us. I also hope that it will allow us to make many other discoveries.

A question of time

In my opinion, I think that my imagination will surely not be realised in a few years, but surely in a little longer time. In any case, I'm going to see for myself in tomorrow's world how all the technology around us is evolving.
