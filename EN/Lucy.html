Lucy - Luc Besson

A representation of the future

Context of the film

The movie Lucy is a movie that is created by French director Luc Besson and released on August 6, 2014, it is a movie that features a young a woman from Taiwan. In this movie, we learn that humans use only 10 percent of their total brain capacity. This girl, gets kidnapped by Chinese mobsters and these traffickers insert a drug into her abdomen.

Superhuman capacity

This drug gives the girl abilities that seem superhuman as it gives her one hundred percent of the total capacity of her brain. Although this film is science fiction, it really makes me imagine that humans may be able to really try to harness the full capacity of the brain.

Science fiction becomes reality.

This film reminds me a bit of Elon Musk's project which wants to implant chips in the brain that can allow us to remotely control computers, but also to cure neurological diseases such as paralysis. This project is sold as revolutionary, but is it really a good thing for human beings, we are not sure. So science fiction films are just films that imagine the future and are made to be made.

A future arrival of this technology.

Perhaps in a few decades we will all have a chip implanted in a part of our body and it will really treat neurological disorders. Tests have already been carried out on monkeys and a paralysed man has already volunteered to test this new chip. What we imagine as the future is finally happening little by little before our eyes, I really wonder how life will be in 30 years.

Filmography of Luc Besson

Le cinquième éléments

Release date 1996

Egypt, 1914. Aliens recover four magic stones, symbols of the four elements, once entrusted to priests. Before leaving, the aliens promise that in 300 years they will bring back the precious stones. In the 23rd century, on their way to Earth, they are annihilated by the planet of evil. The inhabitants of this evil world, the Mangalores, seize the stones and race to Earth.

Le Grand Bleu

Release date 1988

Since childhood, there has been a rivalry between two diving fans, the Frenchman Jacques Mayol and the Italian Enzo Molinari. As adults, they continue to battle it out, descending deeper and deeper in free diving. One day, Jacques meets Johanna, who is working with a professor who has come to study the reactions of a body immersed in ice, and it's love at first sight.

Anna

Release date 2019

Behind her stunning beauty, Anna Poliatova conceals an extraordinary strength and ability that makes her one of the most feared assassins in the world. With her new target in Paris, she sets out on her mission without backup and with the international authorities hot on her trail.
